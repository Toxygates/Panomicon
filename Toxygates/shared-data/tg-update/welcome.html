Toxygates

Toxygates is an integrated platform for toxicogenomics data analysis. For an introduction, please see the user guide, or use the help menu.

Although we will keep maintaining Toxygates, future development will take place under the new name Panomicon, where we emphasise general multi-omics data analysis. A paper about Panomicon has been published in Cell Heliyon. The supplementary materials contain a new guide to data insertion and network analysis. This guide may also be useful to Toxygates users.

A new paper on Toxygates has been published in Scientific Reports. The supplementary information contains a detailed step-by-step guide, which may be helpful.

If you find Toxygates useful in your research, please consider citing either of the above papers.
