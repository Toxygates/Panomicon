Toxygates

Toxygates is an integrated platform for toxicogenomics data analysis. For an introduction, please see the user guide, or use the help menu.

A new paper on Toxygates has been published in Scientific Reports. The supplementary information contains a detailed step-by-step guide, which may be helpful.

The original paper in Bioinformatics may also be helpful.

If you find Toxygates useful in your research, please consider citing either of the above papers.

2018-02-01 news

  • Further UI improvements.
  • Samples now have an associated type (mRNA). This is a preparatory step towards future simultaneous analysis of multiple types of 'omics data.
  • On the "My data" screen, the metadata of samples can now be updated quickly without deleting and re-inserting the batch, and several data uploading bugs were fixed.
Because of a new storage format, if you have any problems displaying charts, please try deleting your sample groups and recreating them again.
