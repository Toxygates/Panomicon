Panomicon

Panomicon is an integrated platform for multi-omics data analysis. For an introduction, please see the user guide (2019 update), or use the help menu.

Panomicon is an evolution of Toxygates. The previous paper was published in Scientific Reports. The supplementary information contains a detailed step-by-step guide, which may be helpful.

The original paper in Bioinformatics may also be helpful.

If you find Panomicon useful in your research, please consider citing either of the above papers.
