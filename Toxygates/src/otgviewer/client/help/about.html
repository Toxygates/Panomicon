Toxygates is being developed by the Mizuguchi laboratory at the National Institutes of Biomedical Innovation, Health and Nutrition (NIBIOHN), Osaka, Japan.

Toxygates is based on data from the Open TG-Gates database.

Toxygates makes use of data and tools from the Bio2RDF project.

Toxygates uses the Ontotext Owlim-SE triplestore.
