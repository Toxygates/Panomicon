Toxygates was developed in the Mizuguchi laboratory at the National Institute of Biomedical Innovation, Osaka, Japan.

Toxygates is based on data from the Open TG-Gates database.

KEGG pathway data and Drugbank targets are obtained from the Bio2RDF project.

Toxygates uses the Ontotext Owlim-SE semantic repository.
